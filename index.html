Biography

I am currently an associate researcher with Academy of Multidisciplinary Studies, , Beijing, China. I graduated from Tsinghua University with a Ph.D degree in mathematics in 2018, under the supervision of Professor Stephen S.-T. Yau. My research interest mainly lies in Nonlinear Filtering and Control theory, Deep learning methods and their applications in image, Radio Frequency(Radar,THz) signal processing.
